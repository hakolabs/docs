# Swap

_Hako_'s Swap page uses the [NEAR Intents Widget](https://docs.near-intents.org/getting-started/what-are-intents) to make cross-chain swaps simple. Instead of manually bridging and swapping step-by-step, you choose the result you want (for example, swapping a native token into USDC), and NEAR Intents routes execution through competing market makers to deliver the best available path.

**Why use it**

* **Cross-chain access**: supports many networks and wallets
* **Price competition**: market makers compete on execution quality
* **Safer execution model**: intents are verified on-chain, and failed swaps are designed to refund to the configured refund address
